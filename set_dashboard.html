🏆 Composite Score — The Primary Decision Metric
0–10 scale · drives all buy, sell & rotation decisions

Every stock gets a single Composite Score (0–10) that blends technical momentum with fundamental quality. This score is the primary metric — it determines which stocks get bought first, which get rotated out, and how strong the portfolio is at any given time.

Formula
Tech Norm = (Tech Score + 3) ÷ 6 × 10 → maps −3…+3 to 0–10
Fund Score = Raw fundamental pts ÷ 13 × 10 → 0–10
Composite = Tech Norm × 60% + Fund Score × 40%
Tech +2, Fund 9.2 (AMATA)
→ (8.33×0.6)+(9.2×0.4) = 8.68/10
Tech +2, Fund 6.2 (JASIF)
→ (8.33×0.6)+(6.2×0.4) = 6.84/10
Tech 0, Fund 8.5 (GFPT)
→ (5.0×0.6)+(8.5×0.4) = 6.40/10 — HOLD
Comp ScoreAction ZoneMeaning
≥ 8.5STRONG BUYTop-tier — buy immediately, highest rotation priority
7.5 – 8.4BUYStrong — buy when slot available, rotation-in candidate
7.2 – 7.4WATCHBorderline — held positions kept, but may be rotated if better stock emerges
6.5 – 7.1WEAKBelow floor (7.2) — qualifies to rotate out; still held if no better option
≤ 6.4SELL / AVOIDWeak — not bought; held positions at high rotation risk
📅 Ex-Dividend Date Guard
If a holding's ex-dividend date is within 14 days, the system delays any SELL or ROTATION until after the record date — protecting dividend income. Stop-loss (−8%) overrides this guard.
⚡ Technical Score (−5 to +5)
5 indicators · gate for all trades

Five indicators each vote +1 or −1 (ADX/Volume may vote 0 in choppy/quiet markets). The total (−5 to +5) is the gate — must clear a threshold before a trade fires. Composite score then ranks priority.

① RSI 14-day — Momentum
RSI < 30 → Oversold → +1  ·  RSI > 70 → Overbought → −1  ·  30–70 → 0
② EMA Trend (EMA20 vs EMA50) — Dual trend
Price > EMA50 AND EMA20 > EMA50 → Both agree bullish → +1
Price < EMA50 AND EMA20 < EMA50 → Both agree bearish → −1
Mixed signal → 0
③ MACD 12/26/9 — Momentum shift
MACD line > Signal line → Building momentum → +1  ·  MACD < Signal → Fading → −1
④ ADX 14-day + Directional Index — Trend strength
ADX > 20 AND +DI > −DI → Trending upward → +1
ADX > 20 AND −DI > +DI → Trending downward → −1
ADX ≤ 20 → Choppy / no trend → 0 (abstain)
⑤ Volume Surge — Conviction check
Volume >1.5× 20-day avg AND price rose → Conviction buy → +1
Volume >1.5× avg AND price fell → Conviction sell → −1
Normal volume → 0

📊 Fundamental Score (0–10)
40% weight in composite · 7 factors · max raw 19 pts

Seven financial metrics scored on graduated tiers (raw 0–19, normalised to 0–10). Covers valuation, profitability, balance sheet quality, earnings momentum, and cash generation.

P/E Ratio · max 3 pts  (valuation)
≤8 → 3 · 8–12 → 2 · 12–15 → 1 · >15 or neg → 0 · N/A → 1
P/BV Ratio · max 3 pts  (asset value)
<1.0 → 3 (below book) · 1.0–1.5 → 2 · 1.5–3 → 1 · >3 → 0 · N/A → 1
ROE · max 3 pts  (profitability)
≥20% → 3 · 12–20% → 2 · 8–12% → 1 · <8% → 0 · N/A → 1
Dividend Yield · max 4 pts  (income)
≥9% → 4.0 · ≥8% → 3.5 · ≥6.5% → 3.0 · ≥5% → 2.5 · ≥3% → 2.0 · any div → 1.0 · none → 0
⚡ NEW: Debt/Equity Ratio · max 2 pts  (balance sheet resilience)
D/E <0.5× → 2 (very low debt) · 0.5–1.0× → 1 · ≥1.0× → 0 · N/A → 1
⚡ NEW: EPS Growth · max 2 pts  (earnings momentum)
≥15% YoY → 2 · >0% → 1 · negative → 0 · N/A → 1
⚡ NEW: FCF Yield · max 2 pts  (cash generation quality)
FCF/Mkt Cap ≥6% → 2 · ≥3% → 1 · <3% → 0 · N/A → 1
ℹ️ Raw score (0–19) ÷ 19 × 10 = Fund score (0–10). Missing data defaults to 1 pt (benefit of the doubt, not zero).
🟢 BUY Criteria
Gate conditions (all must pass)
✅ Tech score crosses to ≥ +3 (was ≤ 0 last scan) — at least 3 of 5 indicators bullish
✅ Stock not already held
✅ Portfolio has < 10 positions
✅ Sector cap: max 2 stocks per sector (BANKING, FINANCE, ENERGY, PROPERTY, INDUSTRIAL, CONSUMER, FOOD, HEALTHCARE, TRANSPORT, TECH, HOTEL)
✅ Liquidity: position size ≤ 10% of stock's average daily trading volume
Fundamental quality gate
✅ P/E < 15  ·  P/BV < 3  ·  ROE ≥ 8%
✅ Paid dividend in last 3 years
Position sizing — volatility-tiered
📐 Base: equal split of available cash ÷ remaining slots
📉 High vol (daily std >3%) → 0.75× base (smaller position, less risk)
📈 Low vol (daily std <1.5%) → 1.25× base (larger position, stable stock)
⚖️ Normal vol → 1.0× base
Priority — ranked by Composite Score
When multiple stocks qualify, highest composite score buys first. Same tech score → better fundamentals wins the slot.
ℹ️ Only fires when score crosses from neutral (≤0) into buy territory (≥+3) — prevents re-buying the same position repeatedly within one session.
🔴 SELL Criteria
Signal sell — tech reversal
⚠️ Tech score drops to ≤ −2 (was ≥ +1 last scan) — at least 2 of 5 indicators bearish
⚠️ Score −1 is a soft warning only — does not trigger a sell
Stop-loss — price-based, automatic
🛑 Price falls ≥ 8% below average cost
🛑 Fires immediately regardless of score or composite — overrides ex-div guard
Rotation — composite score weakness
🔄 Composite score below 6.5 OR tech score ≤ +1, AND a better stock (composite gain ≥ +0.8) is waiting — see Rotation section below
ℹ️ Stop-loss example: bought at ฿100 → auto-sell at ฿92 regardless of any score.
🔄 Rotation Criteria
portfolio full (10/10) · composite score drives selection

When the portfolio is full, the system checks every scan whether any holding should be swapped for a meaningfully better stock. A holding qualifies to rotate out via either trigger below — composite score then determines which pair makes the best swap.

📉 Trigger 1 — Tech Score Fade
Held tech score drops to ≤ +1
At least 4 of 5 indicators no longer bullish
RSI/EMA/MACD/ADX/Volume momentum reversed
Held ≥ 3 days
📊 Trigger 2 — Composite Below Floor
Held composite score < 6.5 / 10
Fires even if tech score is still +2
Catches positions weak on fundamentals
(e.g. high D/E, negative EPS growth, low FCF)
Held ≥ 3 days
✅ Incoming Stock Must Clear
Tech score ≥ +2 (rotation bar, lower than fresh buy ≥+3)
Composite ≥ 7.0 minimum floor
Composite gain ≥ +0.8 over held stock
Not in 5-day cooldown after prior rotation
Sector cap: ≤ 2 per sector still satisfied
3–4 days held: incoming must have comp ≥ 8.5 (fast-track)
5+ days held: comp ≥ 7.0 + gain ≥ 0.8 accepted
🛡️ Anti-churn safeguards
Held loss >5% → protected, no rotation
Max 1 rotation per scan
Max 2 rotations per day
No rotation if regular buy/sell fired same scan
Ex-div within 14 days → skip rotation
ℹ️ Selection order: weakest holding (lowest composite) targeted first. Strongest incoming (highest composite) matched in. Swap fires only if composite gain ≥ +0.8 — small differences never trigger. Sector cap is enforced on incoming stock even during rotation.
